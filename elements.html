A Summary Of My Work

With 6 years in talent roles across scale-ups and start-ups, I’ve consistently exceeded targets. My skillset is in stakeholder/relationship management, data analysis, research project management, process optimisation and business growth.

  • At 18 years old, I became a founding member at CPL Tech UK, an agency with 0 customers or revenue. We scaled to working with brands like TikTok, and I personally contributed over £400k in placement revenue.
  • Next I joined hackajob, a $25m Series B-funded RecTech AI start-up, in a role focused on customer experience. I supported 100+ candidates per week on product utilisation and education, and built scalable internal processes to support that.
  • Following a mass redundancy, I took a part-time tech sales role at Apple, whilst selling job-seeking assistance to friends, families and referrals — which I have continued since then.
  • Contracted at Houseful (Zoopla) as an internal recruiter during a high growth period, focused on stakeholder management, process optimisation, and candidate experience.
  • Now at FirstStage, a bootstrapped AI recruitment product, as the first employee, where I lead all sales and marketing efforts.

I'm passionate about delivering impactful work through building relationships, and have been fortunate to receive many LinkedIn recommendations from clients and colleagues that validate this focus — which can be viewed here.
